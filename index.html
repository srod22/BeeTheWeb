What is Bee The Web All About?

Love them? Fear them? Hate them? Bees are a fascinating species and an important part of today's world. This website tries to bring a new face to what bees do, their purpose as pollinators and how that helps maintain our food supply, and how they have even become a part of human culture for many, many years.

New Scientist informs us of how important Bees mean to humans as well as how intelligent they are. Not only are they excellent pollinators of crops and have an unusual kind of bee known as the honey bee which is an suppliers of honey, but there are also cool facts about how the Queens of a hive are chosen, female bees vs. male bees, how they mate and fertilize new life, and the fact that there are over 20,000 species of bees that we are aware of! But wait! Don't leave this site just yet and learn more from New Scientist's website. This site has some cool, compiled documentation about bees too! Stay awhile.
